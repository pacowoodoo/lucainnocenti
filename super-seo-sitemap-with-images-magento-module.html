SuperSeo Sitemap with images - Module for Magento

What it does

SuperSeo Sitemap improves the way your eCommerce communicates with Google and other search engines.

With this module, you can add to your sitemap:

  • category images
  • product main image and media gallery
  • all image labels are configurable
